Why should we do gym?

We have all heard it many times before - regular exercise is good for you, and it can help you lose weight. you are busy, you have a sedentary job, and you haven't yet changed your exercise habits. The good news is that it's never too late to start. We can start slowly, and find ways to fit more physical activity into your life.
We can get the most benefit those are help we control our weight, reduce our risk of heart diseases, help our body manage blood sugar and insulin levels, help keep our thinking, learning, and judgment skills sharp as we age, strengthen our bones and muscles, improve our sexual health and Increase our chances of living longer.
